About This Product

As time tested as the San Francisco fog, the Cliff House Classic Bloody Mary stands as a monument to simpler times. A delectable accompaniment to our Friday Night Jazz at the Zinc Balcony Lounge, the Cliff House Bloody Mary evokes bold, sweet, spicy, and fruity notes of pepper sauce, molasses, horseradish, and tomatoes, respectively. Perfectly suited for warm meals during cold evenings overlooking the sunset as well as bright Sunday Brunches in our buffet, you'll feel fulfilled and satisfied with this truly Classic taste that has stood the test of time for over 150 years, just like the Cliff House.
